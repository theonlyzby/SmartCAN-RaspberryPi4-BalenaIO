
Namufacturer:	0x01 = DomoCANv3.x

Card Type:	0x50 = 16 Grad
		0x60 = 16 IN

Card Reference:	0 - 254		(Number in DomoCAN)

Card Name:	VarChar(8)

Element Type: 	
		0x11 = OUT 0/1
		0x12 = OUT Analogic

		0x21 = IN Analogic
		0x22 = IN 0/1
		0x23 = OR
		0x24 = AND
		0x25 = NOR
		0x26 = NAND
		0x27 = Rapid Timer (1/10s)
		0x28 = Slow Timer (s)
		0x29 = Counter
		0x2A = Counter-1


Element Number: 0x00 - 0xFF
